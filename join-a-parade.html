Join the Parade

Be part of the vibrant cultural procession at the Kigamboni Utamaduni Carnival

How Would You Like to Participate?

Choose the option that best describes you or your group, then contact our team to finalize the details.

Community & Cultural Groups

Ideal for dance troupes, music ensembles, schools, and neighborhood organizations.

  • Showcase your traditional dances, music, or costumes.
  • Group placement within themed sections of the parade.
  • Access to rehearsal guidance and safety briefings.

Individual Lead Performers

For solo drummers, dancers, storytellers, and other featured artists.

  • Opportunity to lead sections of the parade.
  • Coordination with our artistic director for program flow.
  • Promotion on official carnival channels where applicable.

Schools & Youth Groups

Special focus on involving young people in cultural celebration.

  • Age-appropriate segments and support from our education team.
  • Optional workshops before the event to prepare performances.
  • Discounted costume and equipment rentals where available.

Frequently Asked Questions

Key information about participating in the parade.

Do I need prior performance experience?

No. We welcome both experienced performers and first-time participants. Our team will provide guidelines to help you prepare.

Is there a participation fee?

Most community and cultural groups participate at no cost. Some specialized sections or rental needs may involve fees, which will be discussed during registration.

How do I register my group or myself?

Use the button below to go to our contact form. Select "Parade Participation" as your subject and share details about your performance idea.

Can I rent costumes or instruments for the parade?

Yes. Visit our costume and performance rentals page for available options, or mention your rental needs when you contact us.
