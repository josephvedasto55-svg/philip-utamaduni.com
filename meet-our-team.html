Leadership

Guiding our mission with vision and dedication

Dr. Amina Juma

Director General

With over 20 years of experience in cultural preservation and heritage management, Dr. Amina Juma leads our organization with unwavering commitment to celebrating Tanzania's diverse cultural tapestry. She holds a Ph.D. in Cultural Anthropology from the University of Dar es Salaam and has been instrumental in establishing international partnerships that promote cultural exchange.
