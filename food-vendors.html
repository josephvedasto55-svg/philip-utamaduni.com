Food Vendors

Share your traditional cuisine at our global food festival. Become a vendor and introduce your culture through food.

Why Join as a Food Vendor?

Celebrate Tanzania’s and the world’s flavors while connecting with visitors from across the globe.

What You Can Offer

We welcome a wide range of traditional and contemporary dishes that reflect your cultural heritage.

  • Signature dishes from your home region.
  • Traditional drinks and snacks.
  • Vegetarian and halal-friendly options are encouraged.

Vendor Requirements

To ensure safety and a great guest experience, vendors should be prepared with:

  • Valid food handling permits (where applicable).
  • Clean and well-presented stalls or food trucks.
  • Clear menus with pricing and allergen information.

What We Provide

Registered vendors receive:

  • Allocated stall space within the carnival grounds.
  • Basic utilities information (power/water availability varies by zone).
  • Promotion on our official channels where possible.

Vendor Interest Form

Share your details and our team will contact you with stall availability, pricing, and requirements.
